Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 13, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to BizManager, a product by NextTechTitan. At NextTechTitan, we prioritize your privacy and strive to provide transparency regarding how your information is handled while using our application. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain our practices, specifically regarding the BizManager application.

By using BizManager, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the application.

Information We Do Not Collect

BizManager does not collect or store any personal data, including but not limited to names, addresses, contact information, or financial information. This app is designed to operate offline, ensuring that your business data remains solely under your control.

Data Security Practices

BizManager implements robust security measures to protect the data stored locally on your device:

  • User PINs, security answers, and backup codes are internally encrypted by the app. Encryption and decryption are managed automatically by the app to safeguard your data.
  • We recommend enabling device-level security to further protect your data from unauthorized access.

In the event of device loss or theft, all data stored in BizManager will be inaccessible without your app credentials. However, if the device is lost, the data cannot be retrieved unless you have previously generated and saved periodic reports.

Data Retention

All data entered into BizManager is retained locally on your device for as long as the app remains installed. If you uninstall the app, all data will be permanently deleted.

Data Access and Correction

Since BizManager stores all data locally on your device, you have full control over your information. You can:

  • View your data directly within the app.
  • Generate periodic reports in PDF, Word, or Excel formats for external storage or review.
  • Manually delete individual records or uninstall the app to delete all data.

For assistance with managing your data, please contact our support team.
